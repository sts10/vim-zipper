*zipper.txt*  Quickly Fold and Unfold

Author:  Sam Schlinkert <http://samschlinkert.com/>

Quickly fold and unfold multiple lines of code. Should work
in Visual mode as well as Normal mode. 

                                                *bar*
|                     Create/close fold. Similar to zc.

                                                *Bslash*
\                     Go to next closed fold and open it

                                                *C-Bslash*
<C-\>                 Go to previous closed fold and open it
